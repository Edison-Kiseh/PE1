About us

We are a team of passionate 1st year students in the field of Electronics and ICT in Thomas More Campus De Nayer, Sint-Katelijn-Waver. Together, we came to the conclusion of creating this web page, not only for our project but for future use aswell. Our mission in doing so is to use our experience to make a meaningful difference in the lives of people around the world. We believe that by working together and leveraging our unique talents, we can create a better future for all.

We are committed to honesty, integrity, and transparency in all of our interactions. We believe in treating everyone with respect and empathy, and we strive to create a webshop that'll treat every end user equally and fairly.

We were and are dedicated to create this website and keep it up and running at the best performance. We found this overall challenging with the limited experience as we have technology wise both as individuals and as a team. But this is just another hurdle we have to overcome to become the ICT experts we aspire to be. We believe that by staying curious and open-minded, we can continue to push the boundaries of what is possible and make a lasting impact on the world.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about us. Contact us for more info.
